International Coach / Professional Procedure

  • Enquiry for certification
  • Application link will be send for Certification
  • Filled Application sent by Professional along with profile
  • Application review by Americoteam
  • Application acceptance sent to Professional
  • Payment is made by Professional
  • Online questionnaire is shared with Professional to understand competence
  • Online questionnaire is submitted by Professional
  • Online interview is conducted by panel of experts
  • Certification decision committee meets weekly once to take decision on eligibility of applications
  • Certificate is recommended to Coach
  • Americo& ASCB USA Certificate is shared with Coach
  • Details of Fitness coach along with photo is uploaded on AQSR & ASCB portal
  • Certified Fitness Coach Photo ID Card is given
