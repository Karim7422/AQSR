About Us

Your ISO Certification partner

Americo stands as a distinguished ISO Certification body on a global scale, boasting a specialized cadre of auditors and technical experts proficient in diverse industries such as construction, manufacturing, software, food, and service sectors. Accredited by the esteemed International Accreditation Service (UAF) USA, a prominent member of the International Accreditation Forum (IAF), Americo ensures rigorous audits conducted at client sites. UAF, a reputable non-profit organization established in 1975, operates under the umbrella of the International Code Council (ICC), a renowned leader in the global building construction industry, boasting a membership exceeding 64,000 professionals.

Americo's footprint extends across more than 20 countries worldwide, including the USA, UK, Romania, Singapore, Italy, Peru, Mexico, UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Indonesia, Thailand, Bangladesh, Nepal, Pakistan, and India.

UAF Accredited certification bodies boast accreditation for over 50,000 clients worldwide.

Impartiality Policy

IMPARTIALITY:

Americo is dedicated to upholding impartiality in its provision of management system certification services. We recognize the criticality of impartiality in carrying out certification activities, ensuring the utmost objectivity. Our commitment lies in safeguarding the interests of all parties involved, maintaining independence and impartiality by adhering rigorously to ethical standards.

INDEPENDENCY:

Americo assumes full responsibility and does not outsource decision-making processes related to certification matters, including granting, refusing, maintaining, renewing, extending, reducing, suspending, restoring, or withdrawing certification, as well as expanding or reducing certification scope. Our activities are entirely separate from any organization offering management system consultancy services and are not marketed or associated with such activities. We are committed to rectifying any inappropriate claims made by consultancy organizations suggesting that certification through Americo would be similar, easier, faster, or less expensive. Additionally, Americo has not endorsed any consultancy organization or consultant claiming that certification would be simpler, easier, faster, or less expensive if utilizing their services.
