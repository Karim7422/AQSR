Procedure For ISø Certification in Nigeria

  • Client fills out the application form.
  • Client receives a quotation, and upon agreement, they sign the client agreement.
  • Stage 1 Audit is carried out by an auditor based in Nigeria.
  • Stage 1 Report undergoes review at the Americo office.
  • Stage 2 Audit is performed by an auditor located in Nigeria.
  • Stage 2 Report undergoes review at the Americo office.
  • Certification decision is made by the CDC committee.
  • A softcopy of the certificate is issued to the client.
  • Original hardcopy certificate is dispatched to the client.
